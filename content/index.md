# The Architecture of Immersion

**Introduction**
This guide is designed for junior game designers and students who are transitioning from playing games to building them. Immersion is often treated as a vague buzzword, but this guide deconstructs it into an engineered system. The purpose of this collection is to provide actionable techniques that move beyond abstract theory, allowing designers to solve specific problems in their own projects.
